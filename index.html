About FutureMed

Deep engineering meets medical science.

We are a Spain-based team combining native mobile craftsmanship with applied mathematics and artificial intelligence to create health tools people can trust.

Native medical apps

Expertise in medical application development using native technologies for iOS and Android — fast, reliable and privacy-respecting by design.

ML for medical use

Experience applying early and emerging machine-learning techniques to medical problems, built in Python and validated against real clinical needs.

Mathematics & forecasting

Deep expertise in mathematics for forecasting and modelling — turning noisy, personal health signals into meaningful predictions.

AI, LLMs & RAG

Expertise across AI and ML — including large language models and retrieval-augmented generation — to build assistants that reason over medical knowledge.
